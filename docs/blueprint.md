# **App Name**: Board Bombers

## Core Features:

- Game Initialization: Set up the game with a deck of character and bomb cards, player hands, and initial board states.
- Card Drawing: Allow players to draw cards from the central deck during their turn.
- Card Revealing: Enable players to reveal cards on their own or their opponent's board, triggering bomb explosions and card replacements.
- Card Placement: Implement mechanics for players to strategically place cards on the board, considering card types and their effects.
- Board Management: Manage the game board, including card replacements after explosions and tracking card states (face up or face down).
- Turn Management: Control the flow of the game, handling turn progression, action selection, and enforcing game rules.
- Score Calculation: After a player has flipped a card on their board the tool determines whether there were any score changes, or an explosion.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep blue (#3F51B5) to create a serious and strategic atmosphere, referencing the game's intensity.
- Background color: Light gray (#EEEEEE) to provide a neutral backdrop, allowing game elements to stand out.
- Accent color: Vibrant orange (#FF9800) to highlight interactive elements, explosive actions, and key decisions.
- Body and headline font: 'Inter', a sans-serif font known for its modern, neutral aesthetic, for clear readability.
- Use clear, minimalistic icons for game actions and card types, focusing on functionality over elaborate design.
- Design a clean, card-based layout with distinct areas for the player's hand, board, and game controls.
- Incorporate subtle animations to signal card reveals, explosions, and other significant game events without being too distracting.
